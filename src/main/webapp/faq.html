How to reset my password


Forgotten password: if you cannot log in to your flasher account, please visit www.flasher.com/forgot_password. Enter the email address you use for Flasher, and you will be emailed a link to reset your password. If you forgot your password and you are already logged in to Flasher, first log out of your account, then click on the link.

Change password: you can change your password from your Account > Settings. In order to change your password to a new password, you need to know your current password.

Facebook login: if you created a Flasher account by connecting to Facebook, you were not required to create a password. You can continue to log in via Facebook. If you would like to create a Flasher password, first log out of your Flasher account, then follow the steps as if you forgot your password. Once you create a flasher password, you will no longer be able to sign in using Facebook (but you will still be connected to Facebook in your Flasher account). Instead, you can simply use your email address and newly-created password.

How to bookmark issues when I do not have an account

If you look at the bookmark shape button your top right hand corner of Flasher application, you can use it to add or remove bookmarks. Just click on the golden star to add the current site as a bookmark. Click again on the star to choose one or more labels.

how to customise my view in newsflasher

In our latest version (v 1.1.1) we have used data analytics and machine learning based on your total number of clicks to customise your views. On choosing 'issues' column from column chooser, the issues that you may customise for future use is displayed.

Why do I not see my interested issue in top issue?

Top issues are issues that lots of people are interacting with and sharing via repost, replies, and more. If issues you love aren't showing up as Top issues, it means those issues may not be part of the widespread conversation. Toggle to 'All' results to see more issues matching your query

how are the issues summarised?

We've built an algorithm that finds the facts that have summarises the issue into 3 sentences. issues will refresh automatically and are represented in 3 sentences based on this algorithm. We will have a moderator to ensure that the summary is unbiased and accurate from time to time.

are algorithms reliable in putting top issues in my homepage?

Some older results may be highlighted at the top because they have been determined to be of high value for your query. Our search engines have found those Issues to be particularly relevant to the subject you are looking for, even though they may not be the most recently posted by news sources. Toggle to 'All' to view the rest of your search results in real-time order.

I have searched for something and didn't see any Top results. Why?

This is probably because the query is too specific, or too general, and we might not have enough data to determine any popular results yet. You will still see all relevant real-time issues in your results.

Does issues reappear on homepage?

Yes it does! For some issues may resurface after awhile due to new insights and new biases that arise. We as flashers aim to provide the best and most balanced viewpoint of issues from all social perspective for our valued users like yourself.Give us a hug or donate to Flashers organisation to support our cause of free media :)
